Snakes & Ladders is a game of steep jumps, sudden falls and, of course, chance. Managing your finances isn’t very different. Sometimes there are wads of cash in your wallet, and other times you are broke. The government does its fair bit to contribute or dent your purse through its Budget plans. Roll the dice here and see what came your way, and what didn’t.
